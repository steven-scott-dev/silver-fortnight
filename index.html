Remove OneDoc

Follow these simple steps on your computer.

Step 1 – Click the Start Button

Look at the bottom of your screen and click the Windows button.

Step 2 – Click Settings

When the menu opens, click the gear icon called Settings.

Step 3 – Click Installed Apps

Inside Settings, click Installed apps.

Step 4 – Find OneDoc

Scroll until you see OneDoc.

Step 5 – Click the 3 Dots

Click the three small dots next to OneDoc.

Step 6 – Click Uninstall

Click the Uninstall button when the box appears.

Step 7 – Make Chrome the Default Browser

Go back to Settings → Apps → Default apps.

Step 8 – Set Chrome and Homepage

Choose Google Chrome for HTTP and HTTPS. Then open Chrome settings and set the startup page to: https://www.highland.net
If ads still appear:
  • Restart the computer
  • Remove any apps called OneLaunch, WebDiscover, or Browser Assistant

How to Remove OneDoc

Big, simple steps for Windows 11.

If she gets confused: tell her to do one step at a time. Nothing fancy. Just click what the picture shows.
Start Find OneDoc Remove It Stop Extra Pop-Ups
STEP 1

Click the Start button

Click Settings

Click Installed apps

Find OneDoc
