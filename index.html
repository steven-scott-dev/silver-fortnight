How to Remove OneDoc

Big, simple steps for Windows 11.

If she gets confused: tell her to do one step at a time. Nothing fancy. Just click what the picture shows.
Start Find OneDoc Remove It Stop Extra Pop-Ups
STEP 1

Click the Start button

Tell her: “Look at the bottom middle of the screen and click the Windows button.”

STEP 2

Click Settings

Tell her: “A menu popped up. Click the gear that says Settings.”

STEP 3

Click Installed apps

Tell her: “On the Settings page, click Installed apps.”

STEP 4

Find OneDoc

Tell her: “In the app list, look for OneDoc.”

If she does not see it right away, scroll slowly.

STEP 5

Click the 3 dots

Tell her: “Click the 3 little dots next to OneDoc.”

STEP 6

Click Uninstall again

Tell her: “A box popped up. Click Uninstall.”

OPTIONAL

Stop extra Chrome pop-ups

If ads still show up, open Chrome and turn off bad notification sites.

If ads still keep coming

  • Check for other weird apps like OneLaunch, WebDiscover, or Browser Assistant.
  • Remove those the same way.
  • Restart the computer after uninstalling.
