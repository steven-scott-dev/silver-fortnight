Remove OneLaunch

Follow these steps on your computer.

Step 1 – Click Start

Look at the bottom of your screen and click the Windows button.

Step 2 – Click Settings

When the menu opens, click the gear icon called Settings.

Step 3 – Click Apps

On the Settings screen click Apps.

Step 4 – Click Installed Apps

Click Installed apps.

Step 5 – Find OneLaunch

Scroll until you see OneLaunch.

Step 6 – Click the 3 Dots

Click the three small dots next to OneLaunch.

Step 7 – Click Uninstall

Click the Uninstall button.

Step 8 – Click Start Again

Click the Windows button again.

Step 9 – Click Settings

Click the Settings gear again.

Step 10 – Click Apps

Click Apps.

Step 11 – Click Default Apps

Click Default apps.

Step 12 – Select Google Chrome

Click Google Chrome and make it the default browser.

Step 13 – Set the Home Page

Open Chrome settings and set the startup page to: https://www.highland.net
If ads still appear:
  • Restart the computer
  • Remove apps called Chromium, WebDiscover, Browser Assistant, or Search Manager if you see them
